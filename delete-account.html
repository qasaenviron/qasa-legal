Delete Account & Data Request

App Name: QASA – Quality Air & Safety Assessment

If you would like to request deletion of your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: your-email@example.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – QASA
  3. Include the email address or identifier used within the app (if applicable).

We will process your request within 7–14 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information (if applicable)
  • Associated exposure profiles
  • Optional health survey responses linked to your account

Data That May Be Retained

  • Aggregated and anonymized environmental data that cannot identify individual users
  • Records required for legal or regulatory compliance (if applicable)

Retention Period

Account-linked personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of request approval. Aggregated anonymized data may be retained for research and public health analysis.
