QASA (Quality Air & Safety Assessment) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the QASA mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Environmental Data

  • Air quality measurements (e.g., PM2.5 levels)
  • Time-stamped environmental readings
  • Location-based exposure data

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates (if enabled)
  • Approximate location for air quality mapping

Device Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

Optional Health Survey Data

  • Age group
  • Self-reported respiratory symptoms
  • Duration of exposure

We do not collect personal identifiers such as full names, national identification numbers, or precise home addresses.

2. How We Use Information

  • To calculate environmental exposure risk
  • To generate air quality alerts
  • To improve application performance
  • To support aggregated environmental research

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Aggregated and anonymized environmental data may be shared with research institutions or public health agencies.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect data and use encrypted transmission where applicable.

5. Children's Privacy

QASA is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. User Rights

Users may disable location permissions, uninstall the app at any time, or request deletion of submitted survey data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
